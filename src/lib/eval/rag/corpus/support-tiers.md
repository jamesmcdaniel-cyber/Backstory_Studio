# Backstory Sales AI — Support Tiers

- **Starter** — community forum plus email support, next-business-day response.
- **Growth** — email and chat support, 8-hour response time.
- **Enterprise** — dedicated success manager, 1-hour response time for P1 (critical/production-down) issues, plus a shared Slack channel with the Backstory team.

Response-time targets apply during business hours in the customer's primary region.
