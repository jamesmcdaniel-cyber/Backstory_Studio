# Backstory Sales AI — Discovery Playbook

The discovery motion has four stages: identify, qualify, map stakeholders, propose.

- **Identify** — surface accounts showing buying signals via account scoring.
- **Qualify** — confirm budget, timeline, and a named champion. A deal is "qualified" only when all three are present.
- **Map stakeholders** — chart the buying committee: economic buyer, champion, and blockers.
- **Propose** — deliver a scoped proposal tied to the confirmed budget and timeline.

Rule: a deal without a named champion stays in the qualify stage, regardless of budget or timeline, until a champion is identified.
