# Backstory Sales AI — Onboarding SLA

- **Enterprise** onboarding completes within 30 days of contract signature and includes a named implementation engineer assigned to the account.
- Enterprise kickoff call happens within 5 business days of signature.
- **Growth** and **Starter** customers are self-serve: onboarding happens in-app via guided setup, with no assigned implementation engineer.
- Any customer can reach support during onboarding regardless of tier; only Enterprise gets a dedicated engineer driving the rollout.
