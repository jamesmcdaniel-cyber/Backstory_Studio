# Backstory Sales AI — Competitive Notes

- **vs. Gong** — Gong is primarily a call-recording and conversation-intelligence tool. Backstory instead focuses on forward-looking account scoring and autonomous agents that act on signals, not on recording calls.
- **vs. Clari** — Clari focuses on forecast roll-ups. Backstory adds a graph-RAG correlation layer that connects signals, agent runs, and account facts into one queryable context.
- The primary displacement risk in competitive deals is not another vendor — it is the prospect's homegrown spreadsheets and manual account-review process.
