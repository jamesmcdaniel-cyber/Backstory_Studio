# Backstory Sales AI — Renewal Process

Renewals open 90 days before the contract's term end date.

- The renewal owner is the account's assigned success manager, not the original sales rep.
- Contracts auto-renew for another term unless the customer cancels at least 30 days prior to term end.
- If an account's usage falls below 40% of its purchased seats at any point in the renewal window, a downsell-risk flag is raised automatically.
- The downsell-risk flag routes to the success manager and triggers an outreach task before the renewal is finalized.
